# FAQ Agent - Flux

Ce fichier sert de base de reponses pour un agent qui doit parler de Flux, aider des utilisateurs, ou repondre a des questions simples sur l'app.

Important:
- Ne jamais inventer une fonctionnalite.
- Si une question depasse cette FAQ, repondre honnetement et dire que l'information doit etre verifiee.
- Ne jamais promettre une date de sortie, une synchronisation cloud, une app iPhone/iPad, ou une fonction payante/gratuite si ce n'est pas confirme ailleurs.
- Par defaut, parler de Flux comme d'une app macOS.

## Positionnement

### Q: What is Flux?
A: Flux is a Mac RSS reader designed to help people follow the sources that matter without the noise of algorithm-driven feeds.

### Q: Who is Flux for?
A: Flux is for Mac users who want a calmer way to read news, blogs, creators, and long-form content through RSS.

### Q: What makes Flux different?
A: Flux focuses on clarity, control, local-first reading, and a modern Mac experience instead of noisy social discovery.

### Q: Is Flux a social network?
A: No. Flux is an RSS reader for following your own sources.

### Q: Is Flux only for tech users?
A: No. Flux is useful for anyone who wants to follow websites, blogs, newsletters, or YouTube channels in one place.

### Q: What problem does Flux solve?
A: Flux helps people keep up with their sources without relying on stressful timelines, recommendation algorithms, or endless scrolling.

## Common support questions

### Q: Why can't I add a feed?
A: The URL may not be valid, may not expose a compatible RSS/Atom feed, or may not be available over HTTPS.

### Q: Does Flux accept HTTP feeds?
A: The add-feed flow normalizes toward HTTPS and rejects non-HTTPS feeds in the current app flow.

### Q: Why is my feed not detected automatically?
A: Not every website exposes a detectable feed, and some sites make automatic discovery harder.

### Q: Why are no articles showing?
A: Possible reasons include an empty feed, a failed refresh, a source issue, or no new unread items yet.

### Q: Why is the widget empty?
A: The widget may not yet have article data for the selected feed, or the app may need a refresh first.

### Q: Why is a YouTube source not working?
A: Some YouTube URLs are resolved automatically, but not every format is guaranteed to work perfectly every time.

### Q: Why are some images missing?
A: Some feeds or pages do not expose a usable image, or the source may block image retrieval.

### Q: Why are notifications not appearing?
A: Notifications must be enabled both in Flux and in macOS system permissions.

### Q: Why is the Submit or Save action not working?
A: Ask the user to check that all required fields are filled and that they have granted any needed macOS permissions.

### Q: Where are my saved articles?
A: Saved articles appear in the favorites section.

### Q: Can I delete all my content?
A: Flux includes a delete-all-content style action in its configuration tools.
